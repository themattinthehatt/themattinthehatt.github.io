Welcome!

I'm Matt Whiteway. I'm a fourth year doctoral student in the Applied Mathematics & Statistics, and Scientific Computation Program at the University of Maryland. My research focuses on using techniques from statistics and machine learning to analyze large neural datasets.

I completed a B.Sc. in Physics and a B.A. in Mathematics from the University of Oklahoma. When I'm not trying to understand how brains work I enjoy spending time outside.
