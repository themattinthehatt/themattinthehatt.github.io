Matt Whiteway

Associate Research Scientist, Columbia University

I'm an associate research scientist working in the Zuckerman Institute at Columbia University. I am also a Data Scientist working in the International Brain Lab, a neuroscience collaboration spanning many labs and countries. My research focuses on developing machine learning techniques to analyze large neural and behavioral datasets.

Previously, I was a post-doc with Liam Paninski at Columbia University. I completed my Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics working in the Neurotheory Lab of Dan Butts at the University of Maryland, as well as a B.Sc. in Physics and a B.A. in Mathematics from the University of Oklahoma. When I'm not trying to understand how brains work I enjoy spending time outside, playing around with procedurally generated art, and remaining ignorant of good website design principles. Please have a look around the site!
